About Us

Metro Group is one of the largest manufacturers of ceramic tiles in India. It has an annual aggregate capacity of approx. 36 mn. sq. metres, distributed across 8 units, Mactile India Pvt. Ltd., Metropole Tiles Pvt. Ltd., Metrocity Tiles Pvt. Ltd., Metroworld Tiles Pvt. Ltd., Metrostar Tiles LLP, Metro Ceramics, Panda Ceramics & Ceratec Global Industries in Morbi (Gujarat).

Currently Metro Group produces 1,00,000/- Sq. metre per day. All Manufacturing units of Metro Group are equipped with cutting edge modern technology along with automated facilities, making its mark as one of the best group in Ceramic Industry.
